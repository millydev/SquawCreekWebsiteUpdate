SQuaw creek golf couse

Located in Willow Park, Texas, Squaw Creek Golf Course has been serving the golf community for 48 years. In addition, after the 2015 renovation, Squaw Creek has implemented a training facility on the golf course. This invites all types of golfers that include amateur, intermediate, and professional. Furthermore, the Squaw Creek Gold has had the pleasure of hosing the PGA Tour on four occasions.
